*Biography*

I am an MRes [Systems and Synthetic Biology](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/study/courses/postgraduate-taught/systems-synthetic-biology/){:target="_blank"}
student at the Biopattern Lab, supervised by Dr Ravinash Krishna Kumar and Professor Doryen Bubeck. My research project focuses on engineering bacterial proteins for the controlled delivery of molecules across droplet interface bilayers.

I recently completed my BSc in Medical Biosciences at Imperial. During my final year, I undertook a research placement at AstraZeneca, where I studied how different computational structure prediction methods can be used to model and predict the interaction between the Cereblon-pomalidomide-Zinc Finger complex. I am interested in protein engineering and its application in biomedicine.
