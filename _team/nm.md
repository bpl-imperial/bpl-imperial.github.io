*Biography*

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the BioPattern Lab, where I integrate custom hardware with machine vision software for cell printing. I am also involved in a broader initiative to open-source synthetic cell technologies in collaboration with [b.next](https://bnext.bio/){:target="_blank"}.

I previously completed an MSci in Chemistry with Molecular Physics at Imperial College London, focusing on assigning X-ray scattering spectra using machine learning. I then undertook a PhD in the Membrane Biophysics group at Imperial College London, where I designed custom hardware for drug screening in synthetic biology systems.
